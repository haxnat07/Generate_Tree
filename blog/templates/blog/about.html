About Us

In today's fast-paced world, the memories and stories of our loved ones can often be forgotten or fade away over time. However, The Life Story aims to preserve and share the life stories of deceased individuals, ensuring that their legacy lives on for generations to come.

The Life Story is a web application that allows people to keep the memory of their loved ones alive and offers a platform for capturing and sharing the personal narratives and accomplishments of those who have passed away.

This application was developed for our final year project as part of our Enterprise Computing degree in Dublin City University.
